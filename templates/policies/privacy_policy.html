Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2024

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how DRAW-CLIENT ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our local application.

Information We Collect

We collect the following information from local administrators:

  • Name
  • Email address

How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used for the following purposes:

  • To facilitate connection between the local DRAW-CLIENT and the DRAW server
  • To authenticate local administrators
  • To track and monitor file upload status
  • To communicate important system notifications and updates

Data Storage and Security

Your information is stored locally on your computer. We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information:

  • All data is stored securely on your local system
  • Access is restricted to authorized administrators only
  • Communication with the DRAW server is encrypted

Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy. Your information will be stored as long as you maintain an active administrator account in the system.

Your Rights

As a local administrator, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Update or correct your personal information
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be communicated through the application and will be effective when posted.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the Department of Radiation Oncology, Tata Medical Center, Kolkata.
